About force9 BBS

Originally set up in the 90s with the name "Evolution", it ran on a DEC Intel i386 25mHz, with 1mb of RAM, a 320mb HD and a 56.6k US Robotics modem!

force9.org is a reincarnation of that housed entirely on a Raspberry Pi, and unsurprisingly, is more powerful, has more memory and a lot more storage!
